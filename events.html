IoT Workshop

The objective of the workshop was to give students an insight about the "IOT (Internet of Things)" demonstrated by using Raspberry Pi. Students participated in a team of four. This two day workshop provided an introduction to Raspberry-Pi and IOT, with an emphasis on the smart moving machines of the next generation, i.e., robots, cars, trucks, machine tools, airplanes, satellites, combine harvesters, etc. It also focused on electronic design automation.
"Advance Technology", one of the leading companies with design and development of various embedded software and hardware solutions, organized it.
The event was a success with a total of 82 attendees during the duration. It introduced students with the powerful tools they can use to make something innovative and beneficial to the society.
